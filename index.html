WhatCast

WhatCast is a mobile app developed in React Native that enables the user to discover new podcasts through the 'random' functionality, which will give them a random episode from a random podcast, all just with a single click. Additionally the app also allows the user to search for podcasts they are interested in listening to, or browse the featured categories.

Asobu

Asobu is a social application that features game elements designed for users who are looking to meet new people around them to form friendships and attend events that match their interests. It features a geographic-based system that allows users to search for people interested in short-notice or spontaneous meetups. It allows users to gain experience and level up their profile while earning special badges based on their personalities that other users experience.
